# Statement.md

## Project Title
Attendance Analyzer (Flask Web App)

## Problem Statement
Students often need a quick way to check subject-wise attendance percentages and identify defaulters. Manual calculation from multiple CSVs or Excel files is error-prone and time-consuming.

## Scope
- Upload attendance CSV in supported formats.
- Compute subject-wise attendance percentages.
- Display overall attendance and visual charts.
- Provide simple recommendations (e.g., classes to attend to reach threshold).

## Target Users
- College students
- Class representatives
- Faculty managing attendance

## High-Level Features
- CSV upload and validation
- Subject-wise attendance breakdown
- Overall attendance summary
- Visualizations (bar chart, pie chart)
